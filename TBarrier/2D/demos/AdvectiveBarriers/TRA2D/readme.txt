Computation of TRA [1, 2, 3]

The demos are inside the folder "Main":

_"TRAAgulhas": TRA demo applied to AVISO data set in Agulhas region

Auxiliary functions in current folder:

"TRA": computes the TSE along trajectories.
"extract_vortex": extract vortex from TRA field according to algorithm proposed in [2].

References:

[1] Haller, G., Aksamit, N., & Encinas-Bartos, A. P. (2021). Quasi-objective coherent structure diagnostics from single trajectories. Chaos: An Interdisciplinary Journal of Nonlinear Science, 31(4), 043131.

[2] Encinas-Bartos, A. P., Aksamit, N. O., & Haller, G. (2022). Quasi-Objective Eddy Visualization from Sparse Drifter Data. preprint.

[3] Notebook 5.11. in "Transport Barriers and Coherent Structures in Flow Data" by Prof. George Haller
